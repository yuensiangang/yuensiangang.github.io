## We design and validate new ways to measure learning, health and human decision-making

**ATLAS Group** (Assessment Technologies for Learning & Affective Science) develops behavioural, digital, and computational instruments to capture how people learn, decide, and adapt. We build and validate measures that are rigorous enough for science and practical enough for real-world use in clinics, classrooms, and policy. Our work spans metacognition, developmental screening, motivation and apathy, decision-making, and reinforcement learning, with the goal of turning complex psychological processes into actionable evidence.
